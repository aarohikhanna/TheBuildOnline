About the Show

Not another founder podcast. The real ones.

Most founder podcasts tell you what worked after the dust settles. The Build Story is different — we get in the room while it's still messy. Aarohi sits down with builders mid-journey, not post-exit, to pull out the thinking, the pivots, and the hard lessons that never make the highlight reel.
